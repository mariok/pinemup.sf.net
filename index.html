pin 'em up is a tool for placing small notes/post-its (e.g. TODOs or Reminders) on your desktop and organize them in various ways (categorize, hide, upload, export, ...). Due to platform independence, the notes can also be shared on different operating systems.

 

List of features:

  • OS-independence
  • Integrates into system-tray
  • Free placeable notes
  • Five user-defined categories
  • Fast switch between categories (e.g. "home" and "office")
  • Adjustable font size for each note
  • Make all or single notes stay always on top
  • Automatically save notes to file
  • FTP-upload- and download functions
  • Export notes to a text-file
